Conclusions

  • Two workers provide about 1.94× isolated one-worker throughput and remain the conservative default.
  • The candidate bitboard is substantially slower on both measured production operations, so the array board remains production.
  • PGN parsing allocates heavily but remains below one millisecond for the fixture; optimization is deferred until an import workload crosses a product latency gate.
  • Snapshot load, replay, and cache measurements do not justify mmap or zero-copy complexity.
  • The Phase 3 load test separately enforces interactive completion while historical work remains active.
