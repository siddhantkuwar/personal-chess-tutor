MigrationSchema 1 is explicitly upgraded to schema 2 during validated compaction.
CompactionWrite temporary log, sync, replay-validate, atomically rename, then continue IDs.

Crash semantics

Previously acknowledged events are never discarded. A complete but not-yet-acknowledged record interrupted before sync may be present after restart; consumers therefore use deterministic identities and idempotent projections. A partial record is an unambiguous truncated tail.

Projection accelerators

Periodic JSON snapshots bound startup replay. Derived indexes can be deleted or corrupted and are rebuilt from authoritative events. Memory mapping and zero-copy views were rejected for this release because replay measurements did not show pressure sufficient to justify additional lifetime and bounds complexity.
