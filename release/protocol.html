Local protocols

HTTP binds to 127.0.0.1. JSON bodies cap at 10 MiB and headers at 64 KiB.

MethodPathPurpose
GET/api/healthVersion and local-only readiness.
GET/api/diagnosticsQueue, workers, retries, latency, cache bounds and eviction.
POST/api/import, /api/import/batchBounded PGN or Chess.com ingestion.
GET/POST/api/games/{id}/analysisRead or enqueue analysis.
GET/DELETE/api/jobs/{id}Read or cancel a job.
POST/api/jobs/pause, /api/jobs/resumePersist background queue state.
GET/POST/api/drills/*Review, sessions, hints and verified attempts.
POST/api/drills/supplementalGenerate independently validated optional corpus drills.
GET/api/profile, /api/resources, /api/batchesExplainable projections.
POST/api/storage/snapshot, /api/storage/compactValidated maintenance.

ws://127.0.0.1:8787/ws
{"type":"jobs_snapshot","jobs":[...]}
{"type":"job_update","job":{...}}

The first message is a complete snapshot; subsequent messages are replaceable updates. HTTP remains authoritative after reconnect.
